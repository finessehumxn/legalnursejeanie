The kinds of cases a legal nurse consultant handles

Case types · Reading time ~2 min

A legal nurse consultant is most useful wherever the dispute hinges on medicine. That covers more ground than people expect.

Medical malpractice

The core question is usually whether the care met the standard. Clinical review identifies the deviations and ties them to the record.

Personal injury

Linking the injury to the treatment, and putting the medical impact into context, helps establish both causation and damages.

Nursing home & elder care

Pressure injuries, falls, medication errors, and neglect leave a documentary trail. A nurse knows where to look.

Product & pharmaceutical liability

Connecting a device or drug to a clinical outcome requires reading the chart and the science together.

And more

Workers' compensation, wrongful death, birth injury, and toxic tort cases all benefit from a clinician's read of the evidence.
