Privacy Policy

How Legal Nurse Jeanie, LLC handles the information you share through this website.

Information collected

When you submit the contact form, this website collects the name, email address, phone number, law firm, and message you choose to provide. The site does not require an account and does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

How it's used

The information you submit is used only to respond to your inquiry and to discuss potential legal nurse consulting services. It is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing.

How it's stored

Form submissions are delivered by email and stored only as long as needed to respond to your request and maintain ordinary business records. Reasonable measures are taken to protect this information, though no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Confidentiality

Please do not send privileged, confidential, or protected health information through this form before an engagement and any conflict check are confirmed in writing. Submitting the form does not create a consulting relationship.
