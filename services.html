Core services

How I support your case

Medical record review

A complete read of the chart — identifying what's there, what's missing, and what doesn't add up.

Medical chronology

A clear, dated timeline of care that turns thousands of pages into a story you can use.

Standard-of-care analysis

Whether the care met the accepted clinical standard — and where it deviated, with the record to back it.

Causation & damages support

The clinical questions to ask, the terms to define, and the records to request before you sit down.

Expert testimony

Example scenarios

Where I make the difference

Representative, illustrative situations — not actual case files — showing the kind of problem I solve.

The buried timeline

A delayed-diagnosis case where the answer is hidden in the order of events. I build the chronology that shows exactly when the warning signs appeared and when the response came.

The standard-of-care question

A malpractice matter that turns on whether the care was reasonable. I map the record against the accepted clinical standard and flag each point of deviation.

Records that don't add up

Charting gaps, late entries, or missing documents. I identify what's absent and what to request in discovery before it becomes a problem at deposition.

The damages picture

An injury case where the medical impact needs to be made real. I translate the clinical course into the lasting effects a jury can understand.
